About CynoSurE

Perseverance • Endurance • Commitment

CynoSurE is the premier InterCollege Technical Symposium organized by the Department of Computer Science & Engineering at Priyadarshini Engineering College. Our mission is to create a platform where innovation meets integration, inspiring the next generation of tech leaders.

Join us for an extraordinary event featuring cutting-edge technical competitions, innovative project showcases, and engaging non-technical events that celebrate creativity and technological advancement.
